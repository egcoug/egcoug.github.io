Problem Statement

College students who struggle to form study groups need an easy way to connect with classmates to have access to collaborative learning and academic support.

Affinity Diagram

Affinity diagram with 5 categories, 8 subcategories with respect to the main category and 27 ideas addressing the difficulty of forming study groups in college.

Sketches

The three sketches focus on helping students easily find and join study groups, organize and schedule meetings, and collaborate efficiently through shared resources and progress tracking.

Prototype

The StudyLink prototype features a user-friendly interface that allows students to search, join, or create study groups, view personalized group suggestions, and access tools like group chats and scheduling from a home screen.
